DIGITAL MEDIA PROFESSIONAL, MUSICIAN, WRITER

For Dan Steiny, at the end of the day, it’s really all about the content. The music, the book, the movie that changes everything, the revolutionary podcast, the radio station that informs and inspires. Ever since Dan got his first phonograph at age 10, he has had a passion for content, the creative process that brings it to life and the technologies that allow it to be fully experienced.

This passion has been the driving force behind his digital media career as well as his screenplay and songwriting efforts. He believes that great content not only has the power to entertain, but inform, educate and inspire — to make the world a better place.

Throughout Dan’s career he has had the opportunity to license, distribute, and develop products and partnerships around nearly every type of digital content including music, terrestrial and pure-play streaming radio, online video/film/TV, audiobooks, podcasts, games, mobile apps and software applications. He has also worked with nearly all digital distribution platforms including PC’s, notebooks, tablets, mobile devices, consumer electronics and the connected car. Companies that he’s worked directly for include Sony Electronics, HP, Motorola Mobility, PBS Home Video, Live365, MediaNet, Liquid Audio, Harman International (JBL, AKG, Harman / Kardan), and SoundHound.

On the creative side, Dan has been nominated as finalist and semi-finalist in the Scriptapalooza and Page Award contests for his screenplays KARMA and THE UNSEEN and received “Outstanding Achievement In Songwriting” in the Great American Songwriting contest for his song PAINTED WORLD.

Born and raised in Carmel, CA he’s since lived and worked in New York City and Los Angeles and is currently stirring up trouble in San Francisco/ Silicon Valley. He is a graduate of UCLA.

DIGITAL MEDIA PROFESSIONAL

Licensing – Acquisition – Distribution – Partnerships – Development

Dan Steiny is a digital media business development, product and marketing professional with a passion for all things content – particularly in bridging the gap from traditional media to the world of digital. This passion has given him the opportunity to execute leading edge projects and initiatives for both start-ups and Fortune 500 companies that have led to many profitable, award-winning products and services.

Strategically focused with a constant eye on analytics, revenue and win/win relationships, Dan thrives at the intersection between content and technology. His primary specialties include Business Development, Strategic Partnerships, Business Planning, Digital Licensing/Negotiations (content acquisitions, content distribution, ecomm, digital advertising), Product/Project Management and General Management.

On the content side, Dan has experience with online digital services, terrestrial and pure-play streaming radio, online video/film/TV, audiobooks, podcasts, games, mobile apps and software applications.

On the hardware/distribution side, Dan has expertise with PC’s/notebooks, mobile devices, consumer electronics and the connected car. Companies he’s worked for directly include Motorola Mobility, Sony Electronics, Compaq Computers (HP), Harman International (JBL, AKG, Harman/Kardon), and SoundHound.

Dan enjoys helping define and deliver leading-edge creative digital media / entertainment solutions to market. From the strategy aspect, he can help define and massage the business model. He also has deep personal connections in the industry and knows where to turn for opportunity, support and expansion. He has had the opportunity to personally negotiate and executed more than a hundred licenses with major media, content and consumer electronics companies. From the tactical aspect, he has experience defining the product set, project planning the solution, working with cross-functional teams to handle the integration and managing the day to day aspects to get a project done.

PIANIST – KEYBOARD PLAYER – SONGWRITER

Dan Steiny has been sitting on a piano stool playing ragtime, blues, and writing songs for as long as he can remember.

He received outstanding achievement in songwriting in the Rock / Alt category for his song “Painted World” in the 15th annual Great American Song Contest.

He is currently the keyboard player for San Francisco Americana/folk band, whiskeyhouseband.com.
